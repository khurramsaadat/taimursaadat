About Feasibility Analysis

The feasibility analysis is a crucial component of the design phase for any plan or project. It is conducted to identify the strengths and weaknesses of a proposed project or an existing business. This process is an effective method for assessing potential threats and opportunities in the environment, determining the resources needed to execute the project, and evaluating the likelihood of the project's success.
